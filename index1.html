About Amazon

Amazon is an American multinational technology company that focuses on e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming, artificial intelligence, and more. Founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994, Amazon started as an online bookstore and has grown into one of the world's largest online retailers.

Our Products

Amazon offers a wide range of products, including electronics, books, fashion, and more. With Amazon Prime, customers enjoy fast shipping, exclusive access to movies, TV shows, ad-free music, and more.
